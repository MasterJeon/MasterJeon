## 🌱 What Drives Me

</div>

I care about **sustainable software development**. Not just building things that work, but building things that are **responsible**.

My final year project **EcoSelect** asks a question most developers never think to ask:
> *What does this AI model cost the planet?*

It benchmarks CNN compression techniques against energy consumption and recommends the most carbon-efficient option without sacrificing accuracy. Alongside **GreenAI**, a platform that maps real-time carbon intensity of cloud data centres, this reflects my genuine commitment to technology that considers its environmental footprint.
